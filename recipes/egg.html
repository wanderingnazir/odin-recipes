Nazir's Famous Egg Sandwhich

Description

No need to over complicate a simple dish like this, this is what you prepare when you're a busy person, you're in a hurry, you want something nutricious, filling and quick to make.

Ingredients

  • 2x Medium Eggs
  • 2x Slices of cheese
  • 1x Tomato
  • 1x Lettuce
  • 1x Onion
  • 2x Slices of Bread

Preparation

  1. Start by dicing your onion and cracking your eggs in a bowl
  2. repeteadly beat the eggs until homogenous
  3. warm up your pan up to medium heat and toast the two slices of bread on both sides
  4. after that take it down to low heat, add more oil and stir the onions in it for a few minutes
  5. gently pour the beaten eggs into the pan, add salt and pepper then close the lid to allow the residual heat to cook the egg from the top
  6. put the cooked eggs out onto a plate and start layering the ingredients
  7. put the cheese first, then the eggs, then the sliced tomatoes and a few leaves of lettuce and the second slice of toast
  8. wrap your sandwhich up and head out into the world
